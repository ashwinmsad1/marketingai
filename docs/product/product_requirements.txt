# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## AI Marketing Automation Platform

---

## 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

### Vision Statement
Transform the Indian small business marketing landscape by democratizing AI-powered creative generation and Meta advertising automation, making professional marketing accessible to businesses of all sizes.

### Mission
Replace expensive creative teams (₹15K-75K/month) with intelligent AI automation (₹999-₹5,999/month) while delivering superior results through data-driven optimization and culturally-aware creative generation.

### Target Market
**Primary**: Indian small to medium businesses (SMBs) spending ₹10K-100K monthly on digital marketing
**Secondary**: Marketing agencies serving Indian SMBs
**Tertiary**: International businesses targeting Indian markets

### Value Proposition
- **90% Cost Reduction**: From ₹50K/month creative teams to ₹3K/month AI automation
- **10x Faster**: Campaign creation in 30 minutes vs 3+ days
- **Culturally Intelligent**: AI trained on Indian market nuances and seasonal patterns
- **Autonomous Optimization**: Self-improving campaigns with minimal human intervention

## 2. CORE FEATURES & REQUIREMENTS

### 2.1 Creative Studio (Standalone)
**Priority**: P0 (Must Have)

**User Story**: "As a business owner, I want to generate professional marketing creatives without needing Meta account connection, so I can create content for any platform."

**Core Features**:
- **Independent Functionality**: Works completely without Meta account connection
- **Simple Generation Interface**: Prompt → Create → Save to library
- **Personal Creative Library**: All generated assets stored for user access
- **Campaign Integration**: Choose from library OR auto-generate during campaign creation

**Image Generation (Google Imagen 4.0)**:
- **Formats**: 1:1 (social posts), 16:9 (banners), 9:16 (stories), 4:5 (mobile)
- **Styles**: Professional, modern, traditional, festive, minimalist
- **Indian Elements**: Festivals, regional preferences, cultural awareness
- **Brand Integration**: Apply analyzed brand voice and colors

**Video Generation (Google Veo 3.0)**:
- **Durations**: 15s, 30s, 60s
- **Formats**: Square, landscape, portrait, stories
- **Motion Styles**: Dynamic, subtle, static overlay
- **Audio**: Background music, voiceover support options

**Asset Library Management**:
- **Organization**: Tags, campaigns, format, date-based sorting
- **User Actions**: Favorite marking, usage tracking, bulk operations
- **Search & Filter**: Advanced search across all user assets
- **Export Options**: Multiple format downloads

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Generate image in under 30 seconds
- Generate video in under 60 seconds
- Support minimum 10 concurrent generations per user
- 99% uptime for Creative Studio features
- Export assets in multiple formats (PNG, JPG, MP4, GIF)

### 2.2 Brand Voice Analysis
**Priority**: P0 (Must Have)

**User Story**: "As a business owner, I want the AI to understand my brand voice from my website, so all generated content maintains consistency with my existing brand."

**Requirements**:
- **Website Scraping & Analysis**:
  - Extract brand voice, tone, messaging patterns
  - Identify color palette and typography preferences
  - Analyze value propositions and key messaging
  - Extract logo and visual brand elements

- **Social Media Intelligence**:
  - Analyze existing Facebook/Instagram posts
  - Identify engagement patterns and successful content types
  - Extract hashtag strategies and audience preferences

- **Brand Guidelines Generation**:
  - Auto-generate comprehensive brand guidelines
  - Provide content suggestions based on analysis
  - Create brand-specific prompt templates

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Complete website analysis in under 5 minutes
- Extract minimum 15 brand attributes with 90%+ accuracy
- Generate usable brand guidelines automatically
- Support analysis of websites in English and Hindi

### 2.3 Multi-Purpose Campaign System
**Priority**: P0 (Must Have)

**User Story**: "As a business owner, I want to create different types of campaigns based on my business goals, so I can drive specific outcomes like leads, sales, or brand awareness."

**Campaign Types**:
1. **Lead Generation**: Forms, contact capture, consultation bookings
2. **Sales & E-commerce**: Product sales, online store traffic
3. **Brand Awareness**: Reach, impressions, brand recognition
4. **App Downloads**: Mobile app installs and engagement
5. **Event Promotion**: Event attendance, registrations
6. **Store Visits**: Physical location visits, foot traffic
7. **Retargeting**: Customer retention, repeat purchases

**Requirements per Campaign Type**:
- Purpose-specific asset generation templates
- Optimized targeting recommendations
- Industry-specific best practices integration
- Budget allocation suggestions based on goals
- Success metrics definition and tracking

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Support all 7 campaign types with unique workflows
- Provide industry-specific templates for 10+ sectors
- Auto-suggest budget based on campaign purpose and audience size
- Generate campaign in under 30 minutes from start to launch

### 2.4 Meta API Integration
**Priority**: P1 (Should Have)

**User Story**: "As a business owner, I want to connect my Meta account and automatically launch campaigns, so I don't need to manually recreate everything in Meta Ads Manager."

**Requirements**:
- **OAuth Authentication**: Secure Meta account connection
- **Campaign Creation**: Direct campaign launch to Meta platform
- **Real-time Sync**: Bidirectional data synchronization
- **Performance Tracking**: Automated metrics collection
- **Budget Management**: Real-time budget monitoring and alerts

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Complete OAuth flow in under 2 minutes
- Successfully create campaigns with 95%+ success rate
- Sync performance data every 4 hours maximum
- Support multiple ad accounts per user
- Handle Meta API rate limits gracefully

### 2.5 AI-Powered Optimization
**Priority**: P1 (Should Have)

**User Story**: "As a business owner, I want my campaigns to automatically improve themselves, so I achieve better results without constant monitoring."

**Optimization Features**:
- **Budget Optimization**: Automatic budget reallocation based on performance
- **Audience Expansion**: Lookalike and interest expansion when profitable
- **Creative Rotation**: A/B testing and winning creative promotion
- **Bid Strategy**: Automated bid adjustments for target metrics
- **Schedule Optimization**: Best time/day analysis and adjustment

**Requirements**:
- Minimum 7-day data before optimization triggers
- Maximum 50% budget increase per optimization
- Notification system for all optimization actions
- Rollback capability for failed optimizations
- Performance tracking for optimization impact

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Achieve 15%+ performance improvement through optimization
- Execute optimizations within 24 hours of trigger conditions
- Maintain detailed logs of all optimization actions
- Support user-defined optimization constraints

## 3. USER EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Onboarding Flow
**Duration**: Maximum 10 minutes for complete setup

**Steps**:
1. **Account Creation** (2 minutes)
   - Email/password registration
   - Subscription tier selection
   - Payment setup (for paid tiers)

2. **Business Profile Setup** (3 minutes)
   - Business type and industry selection
   - Website URL and social media links
   - Target audience preferences
   - Monthly marketing budget range

3. **Brand Voice Analysis** (5 minutes)
   - Automated website scraping and analysis
   - Brand guidelines generation
   - User review and customization of extracted data

**Success Metrics**:
- 80%+ onboarding completion rate
- Under 10% drop-off at any single step
- 90%+ user satisfaction with brand analysis accuracy

### 3.2 Dashboard Experience
**Primary Actions**:
- Quick campaign creation (1-click templates)
- Performance overview with key metrics
- Recent activity and AI optimization alerts
- Creative Studio access and asset management

**Layout Requirements**:
- Mobile-responsive design for Indian smartphone users
- Hindi language support for key interfaces
- Dark mode support for extended usage
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)

**Performance Requirements**:
- Page load times under 2 seconds
- Real-time metric updates
- Offline capabilities for creative review

### 3.3 Creative Studio Interface
**Workflow**:
1. **Prompt Input**: Natural language creative brief
2. **Style Selection**: Brand-aware style options
3. **Format Selection**: Multiple aspect ratios
4. **Generation**: Real-time progress tracking
5. **Review & Edit**: Basic editing capabilities
6. **Export/Save**: Multiple format options

**Requirements**:
- Drag-and-drop asset organization
- Bulk generation capabilities (up to 10 assets simultaneously)
- Real-time collaboration for team accounts
- Version history and asset comparison
